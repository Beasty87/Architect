Aleksandra Nikolic

Founded in 2005, Rethink Design Studio is a design studio based in Savannah, owned by husband-wife duo Joel and Erika Snayd. We specialize in environments for the modern dweller, pushing the boundaries of design to create a space influenced by our client’s wants and needs. Our signature aesthetic pairs historical structure and building integrity with the influence of purist style, producing a creative environment that’s smart, well-balanced and inspired. With more than thirteen years of commercial and residential experience, we are fluent in the language of all design, construction, and project management, guaranteeing our clients a space that not only reflects their values, but is a marriage of beautiful form and everyday function. From the first meeting to the reveal, our process ensures a tailored, one-of-a-kind experience that is uniquely yours.
